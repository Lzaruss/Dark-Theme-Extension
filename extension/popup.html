About Me

I am a dark theme extension for Google Chrome that allows you to easily switch between light and dark themes on websites and web apps. With a simple click of a button, you can toggle between themes and customize your browsing experience to your preference. Whether you prefer a light theme for reading during the day or a dark theme for a more comfortable night-time browsing experience, I have you covered.

Features:

  • Easy toggle between light and dark themes
  • Customizable color scheme
  • Option to automatically switch between themes based on time of day
